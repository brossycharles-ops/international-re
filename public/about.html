About International RE

A global perspective on Latin American real estate — built through first-hand experience.

Why We Started This

International RE was born out of a simple idea: the best real estate insights come from people who've actually been there. After visiting 30+ countries across 4 continents, our founder saw a gap — smart, accessible information about Latin American real estate markets was almost impossible to find.

Most people hear about Costa Rica or Argentina and wonder: "Can I actually buy property there? Is it safe? What do prices look like?" We answer those questions every week with real data, legal context, and on-the-ground insights.
