Preview: Month-by-Month Relocation Checklist

Showing months 6 and 5. Subscribe to unlock the full 47-item checklist.

6 Months Before Moving

  • Research 3 target countries using our comparison table — narrow to 1–2 finalists
  • Calculate your minimum viable monthly budget for each target location
  • Check your passport expiration — renew if under 18 months remaining
  • Identify which visa category you qualify for in each target country
  • Open a Charles Schwab or Wise account — both have zero foreign transaction fees
  • Start a 6-month expense audit: know exactly what you spend and on what

5 Months Before Moving

  • Book a 2–4 week "test run" trip to your top target city
  • Consult a cross-border tax advisor (US/Canada expats: FBAR, FATCA, Foreign Earned Income Exclusion)
  • Get an international health insurance quote (SafetyWing, Cigna Global, BUPA International)

4 Months Before & Beyond — Locked

  • Apostille your key documents (birth certificate, marriage certificate, diplomas) — takes 2–6 weeks
  • Notify your bank of upcoming international address change to avoid account freezes
  • Research and shortlist 3 local attorneys in target country for property or visa work
  • Begin gathering visa application documents: proof of income, police clearance, medical exam
  • Decide: sell, store, or ship your belongings — get quotes from international movers
