Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last updated: April 2026

Who we are

International RE (internationalre.org) is an independent newsletter covering Latin American real estate markets. We are not a real estate broker, agent, or investment adviser.

What information we collect

We collect only what you voluntarily provide:

  • Your email address when you subscribe to the newsletter
  • Your first and last name, if you choose to provide them
  • The page or form you subscribed from (to understand which content is useful)

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or sensitive personal data.

How we use your information

  • To send you the weekly International RE newsletter
  • To send you the 2026 Market Entry Guide on signup
  • To send occasional follow-up emails in the days after signup (a short drip sequence introducing key markets)
  • To understand which pages and topics drive signups, so we can improve the site

We never sell your email address. We never share it with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Email service provider

We use Resend (resend.com) to send email. Your email address is stored in a secure file on our server and used solely to deliver the newsletter. Resend processes data in accordance with GDPR.

Your rights

You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any email, or by visiting internationalre.org/unsubscribe.html. After unsubscribing, we will not contact you again.

If you want your data deleted entirely, email us at hello@internationalre.org and we will remove your record within 7 days.

Cookies and analytics

We do not use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking. We may use basic server-side analytics (page views, referral sources) to understand traffic patterns — this data is aggregated and not tied to individuals.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact

Questions? Email hello@internationalre.org.
