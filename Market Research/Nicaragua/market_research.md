# Nicaragua — Real Estate Market Research (2015–2025)

## Market Overview
Nicaragua's market peaked in 2018, crashed during political unrest, and has been on a slow recovery. By 2025, coastal infrastructure investment (the Emerald Coast Highway) is driving renewed interest from foreign buyers — especially Canadians and Europeans drawn by prices 40–60% below Costa Rica.

## Price Per Sqm (2025)
- Apartments: ~$1,230/sqm
- Houses: ~$930/sqm
- Condos: $90K–$600K+ range
- Managua rentals: 8–11% yield
- Emerald Coast / Tola / Rivas: +12% annual appreciation (top performer)
- Granada colonial: +7% annual appreciation

## Single-Family Homes
- San Juan del Sur: $50K–$300K (beachfront lots from $30K–$80K)
- Granada colonial homes: under $200K
- Tola/Emerald Coast: fastest appreciation in country

## Foreign Buyer Rules
Foreigners have constitutional equal rights to property. No residency needed. Investing $50K+ qualifies for investor residency visa. Use local attorney for title due diligence.

## Outlook
4–7% national growth forecast through 2026. Top 5-year appreciation zones: Rivas coastal belt, Managua prime corridors, Granada colonial core (35–50% cumulative).
