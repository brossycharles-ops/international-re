# Argentina — Real Estate Market Research (2015–2025)

## Market Overview
Argentina's most volatile market in Latin America. Prices peaked ~2018, fell ~50% in real USD terms by 2023, and are now recovering strongly. All transactions in USD cash. As of 2026, Buenos Aires apartments are still 40–50% below their 2018 USD peak — representing a unique entry point. Mortgage activity exploded +1,042% in 2025 as rates dropped from 130% to 29%.

## Price Trends
- 2018–2023: -50% in real USD
- 2024–2025: recovering, +5.6% USD YoY
- Premium neighborhoods: forecast +8–12% in 2026
- Emerging areas (Chacarita, San Telmo): up to 10–15% annually

## Rental Yields
- 3–5% (established areas — low compared to region)
- Short-term Airbnb in tourist neighborhoods: significantly higher
- Best for capital gains play, not cash flow

## Foreign Buyer Rules
No restrictions. Just need a CDI (tax ID). Notary (escribano) handles closing. Budget 6–9% above purchase price for closing costs. Cash transactions standard.

## Outlook
Forecast +5–8% USD appreciation nationally in 2026. Premium areas (Palermo, Recoleta) +8–12%. Still 40–50% below historical peak in real terms = strong upside potential.
