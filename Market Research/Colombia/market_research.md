# Colombia — Real Estate Market Research (2015–2025)

## Market Overview
Colombia's housing prices have increased ~91% nominally over the decade (~12% real after inflation). The peso dropped ~40% vs USD since 2015, making Colombia exceptionally affordable for dollar buyers. Foreign buyers now represent 15–20% of the national market. Medellín leads growth; Cartagena leads short-term rental yields.

## Foreign Buyer Rules
No restrictions. Foreigners buy with passport + Colombian tax ID (NIT). Simple process with local notary. No capital gains tax on properties held 2+ years (primary residence).

## Outlook
Strong continued growth led by Medellín. Currency depreciation makes Colombia one of the best-value markets for USD buyers in all of South America.
