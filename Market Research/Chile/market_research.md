# Chile — Real Estate Market Research (2015–2025)

## Market Overview
Chile is Latin America's most stable, transparent real estate market — #1 in economic freedom in the region. Steady 4–6% annual appreciation without boom-bust cycles. Foreign buyers can obtain mortgages (up to 60–70% LTV), unlike most of Latin America. Santiago leads urban demand; the Lake District (Pucón, Villarrica, Puerto Varas) attracts lifestyle/vacation buyers.

## Appreciation
- Steady +4–6% annually (last 10 years, real terms)
- No major crash cycles vs neighbors
- Santiago appreciation led by supply constraints in premium areas

## Rental Yields
- Santiago premium: 3–5%
- Coastal / vacation: 5–7%
- Lake District Airbnb: seasonal but strong

## Foreign Buyer Rules
Foreigners have full ownership rights. Unique in Latin America: banks offer mortgages to foreign buyers. Transparent legal system, public notary required. No restrictions on repatriating capital.

## Outlook
Continued stable 4–6% appreciation forecast. Safest risk-adjusted market in Latin America for long-term holders.
