# Brazil — Real Estate Market Research (2015–2025)

## Market Overview
After a massive pre-2015 boom (São Paulo +224%, Rio +266% from 2008–2015), Brazil corrected sharply in 2016–2019 before rebounding 45% since 2020. 2025 marks the 4th consecutive year of above-inflation growth. Coastal secondary cities (Balneário Camboriú, Salvador, João Pessoa) are now the fastest-appreciating markets — some posting 17–21% annually.

## Foreign Buyer Rules
No restrictions for foreigners. Must obtain CPF (taxpayer ID). Purchase through notary (Cartório). Transfer tax (ITBI) ~2–3%. Mortgages available for foreigners in some banks.

## Outlook
8–12% annual appreciation in major markets through 2026. Coastal cities: 15% potential. High Selic rate (14.75%) limits mortgage buyers but cash/foreign buyers unaffected.
