# Costa Rica — Real Estate Market Research (2015–2025)

## Market Overview
Costa Rica's property market has grown roughly 60% in local currency over the past decade (~35% real terms after inflation). The pandemic sparked a dramatic surge — especially on the Pacific coast — with Guanacaste prices rising 400% between 2020–2023 before a 36% correction in 2024.

## Condos

- Beachfront condos: $150K–$400K

## Rental Yields
- Beach towns: 7–10%
- San José: ~8.25%
- Foreign buyers: ~40% of all transactions

## Foreign Buyer Rules
Foreigners have identical property rights to citizens. No partner, no permit needed. Annual property tax: 0.25% of registered value. No capital gains tax on primary residence.

## Outlook
Continued appreciation driven by digital nomads, retirees, and eco-tourism. Coastal corrections expected to stabilize by 2026.
