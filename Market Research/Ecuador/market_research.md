# Ecuador — Real Estate Market Research (2015–2025)

## Market Overview
Ecuador uses the USD, eliminating currency risk. Cuenca is the top expat destination (consistently ranked #1 retirement destination globally). Quito is the capital urban market. Salinas is the main Pacific coastal resort town. Prices are among the lowest in South America for comparable quality.

## Price Per Sqm (2025)
- Quito premium (Gonzalez Suarez, La Floresta): ~$1,200–$2,000/sqm
- Quito standard: ~$700–$1,200/sqm
- Cuenca: ~$800–$1,500/sqm (extremely affordable)
- Salinas (Pacific coast): ~$1,000–$2,000/sqm
- Manta (coastal city): ~$600–$1,200/sqm

## Rental Yields
- Quito: 5–7%
- Cuenca: 4–6%
- Coastal vacation: 6–10% (seasonal)

## Foreign Buyer Rules
No restrictions. USD economy eliminates currency risk. Simple process with notary. Visa options for retirees ($800/month income requirement). Very expat-friendly tax environment.

## Outlook
Stable, affordable market. Strong demand from North American retirees. Cuenca and coastal areas growing due to expat community expansion.
