# Belize — Real Estate Market Research (2015–2025)

## Market Overview
Belize is the only English-speaking country in Central America, making it uniquely accessible for American buyers. Ambergris Caye is the #1 market — a Caribbean island with the world's second-largest barrier reef. Prices are in USD. A new $500K Investor Fast-Track to Permanent Residency was approved in December 2025.

## Price Per Sqm / Property Ranges (2025)
- Ambergris Caye beachfront condos: $250K–$1.5M+
- Ambergris Caye inland: $100K–$400K
- Placencia (southern coast): $150K–$800K
- Belize City: $80K–$250K (more affordable)
- Caye Caulker: $100K–$350K

## Rental Yields
- Ambergris Caye short-term rental: 8–15% in peak season
- Annual yields: 6–10%
- Barrier Reef proximity = premium tourism demand year-round

## Foreign Buyer Rules
No restrictions on foreign ownership. USD-denominated market. English legal system (British common law). Title insurance available. Stamp duty ~5% on transfer.

## Outlook
Growing investor interest driven by new residency fast-track program. Ambergris Caye remains the primary market. New cruise tourism infrastructure boosting Belize City demand.
