# Panama — Real Estate Market Research (2015–2025)

## Market Overview
Panama's property market has grown over 120% from 2015 to 2025 (45% in nominal terms since 2016, ~30% inflation-adjusted). Panama City is dominated by high-rise condos (70% of listings). Premium areas like Punta Pacifica and Casco Viejo lead at $4,000+/sqm. Beach destinations (Bocas del Toro, Pedasí) offer 6–12% rental yields.

## Condos
- 70% of Panama City residential listings are high-rise condos
- New builds command 15% premium over resale
- Boquete/El Valle SFH: $100K–$400K range

## Rental Yields
- Bocas del Toro, Pedasí, Playa Venao: 6–12% (vacation rental)
- Panama City apartments: avg rent $12.96/sqm/month
- City rents up 12% YoY in prime areas (2025)

## Foreign Buyer Rules
No restrictions on foreign ownership. Pensionado program offers excellent retirement benefits. Panama uses USD — no currency risk.

## Outlook
1–3% nominal annual appreciation forecast for 2026. Moderate growth with some oversupply in mid-range condo corridor. Beach/mountain markets outperforming.
