# Peru — Real Estate Market Research (2015–2025)

## Market Overview
Lima is the primary market, driven by a growing middle class and urbanization. Miraflores and San Isidro are the prime expat/investor districts with strong rental demand. Cusco offers a niche heritage-tourism market. Peru's economy has shown resilience, making it an increasingly attractive mid-tier entry point in South America.

## Price Per Sqm (2025)
- Lima Miraflores (premium): ~$2,000–$3,000/sqm
- Lima San Isidro: ~$2,200–$3,500/sqm
- Lima emerging districts: ~$1,000–$1,500/sqm
- Cusco (heritage): niche/boutique pricing

## Rental Yields
- Lima Miraflores: 5–7%
- Airbnb in Cusco: seasonal, 8–12% in peak periods

## Foreign Buyer Rules
No restrictions on foreign ownership. Need RUC/DNI number equivalent. Notarial process required. No capital gains tax for individuals. Simple, transparent process.

## Outlook
Stable growth market. Lima continues to urbanize rapidly. Tourist markets (Cusco, Sacred Valley) offer boutique investment opportunities.
