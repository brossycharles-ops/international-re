# Guatemala — Real Estate Market Research (2015–2025)

## Market Overview
Antigua Guatemala is the crown jewel of the Guatemalan market — a UNESCO-recognized colonial city with cobblestone streets, volcano views, and a thriving expat/digital-nomad community. Guatemala City is the urban commercial hub. Lake Atitlán offers a growing boutique property market. Prices remain significantly below comparable colonial cities like Cartagena or San Miguel de Allende.

## Price Ranges (2025)
- Antigua colonial homes (restored): $150K–$1M+
- Antigua condos/apartments: $80K–$350K
- Guatemala City (Zona 10, 14, 15 premium): $1,200–$2,500/sqm
- Lake Atitlán: $80K–$500K (land + homes)
- Pacific coast (Monterrico): $50K–$200K

## Rental Yields
- Antigua short-term: 8–14% (strong tourism/expat demand)
- Guatemala City long-term: 5–7%
- Lake Atitlán: 6–10% (boutique tourism)

## Foreign Buyer Rules
No restrictions. Foreigners buy with passport. Use local attorney. Title insurance available. Transfer tax ~3.5%. Guatemala City and Antigua have reliable title registries.

## Outlook
Growing interest from North American and European expats. Antigua remains the top market. Lake Atitlán gaining traction. Still undervalued vs comparable colonial cities in Mexico/Colombia.
