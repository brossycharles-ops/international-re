# Uruguay — Real Estate Market Research (2015–2025)

## Market Overview
Uruguay is Latin America's most stable and investor-friendly market. Cumulative 20–45% real growth over the past decade. Foreign buyers — especially Argentines (75%) and Brazilians (20%) — dominate the luxury coastal segment. Punta del Este is one of South America's premier luxury destinations. Residency available with $525K+ purchase + 60 days/year presence.

## Price Per Sqm (2025)
- New houses nationally: ~$2,244/sqm (USD)
- Punta del Este luxury: $2,000–$10,000+/sqm
- José Ignacio (ultra-luxury): among highest in South America
- Montevideo Carrasco: $2,000–$3,500/sqm
- Montevideo emerging: $1,200–$1,800/sqm

## Appreciation
- Coastal areas (Punta del Este, José Ignacio): 8–12% annually
- Premium coastal segments: $2M–$20M properties common
- National forecast: +3–5% (next 12 months), +6–10% for coastal premium

## Rental Yields
- Long-term: 4–6%
- Punta del Este high season: strong short-term yields
- Foreign buyers 30–66% of premium coastal transactions

## Foreign Buyer Rules
No restrictions. Same rights as citizens. Tax incentives for promoted developments. Invest $525K+ → residency with 60 days/year. No capital gains tax on appreciated properties held 3+ years.

## Outlook
Most risk-stable market in Latin America. Growth tied closely to Argentine economy (75% of foreign buyers are Argentine — a risk and an opportunity).
