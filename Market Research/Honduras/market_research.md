# Honduras — Real Estate Market Research (2015–2025)

## Market Overview
Roatán (Bay Islands) is Honduras's premier international real estate market — a Caribbean island with crystal-clear water, a barrier reef, and prices significantly below comparable Caribbean markets. Growing expat community. Infrastructure improving steadily. Mainland cities (Tegucigalpa, San Pedro Sula) offer urban opportunities at low entry points.

## Price Ranges (2025)
- Roatán beachfront homes: $200K–$1.5M+
- Roatán condos: $100K–$500K
- Roatán lots: $30K–$300K (location dependent)
- Tegucigalpa (city): $50K–$300K
- La Ceiba (Caribbean coast, mainland): $60K–$250K

## Rental Yields
- Roatán vacation rental: 8–14%
- Year-round dive tourism = strong demand
- Property management widely available (Steve Hasz's company is island's largest)

## Foreign Buyer Rules
Foreigners can own property outright. Must use a local attorney. Corporations often used for property holding. No restrictions on repatriating proceeds. Residency available with $50K investment.

## Outlook
Roatán continues growing as Caribbean real estate prices in other islands (Cayman, USVI, BVI) push buyers toward more affordable alternatives. Strong long-term appreciation potential.
